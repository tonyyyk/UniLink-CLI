    Help & Service Manual

    🚀 Getting Started

    1. Register a new account with your username, password, and major.
    2. Log in with your credentials.
    3. Go to My Profile and add your Strengths and Weaknesses.
    4. Use Find Partners to discover compatible study partners.

    🔍 Finding Study Partners

    Two matching strategies are available:

    • Complementary Skills — finds partners whose strengths cover your weaknesses and vice versa. Best for balanced collaboration.
    • Same Major — prioritises students in the same field with shared knowledge areas.

    Higher match scores mean better compatibility. Click Message on any result to start a conversation.

    💬 Messaging

    • Go to Messages in the sidebar.
    • Click any contact to open the conversation.
    • A red badge shows how many unread messages you have from each contact.
    • Messages refresh automatically every 3 seconds when a conversation is open.
    • To contact an admin directly, find admin in the contacts list and send a message.

    👥 Study Groups

    • Go to Study Groups to browse all groups.
    • Click Join to become a member of any group.
    • Click + Create Group to start your own group with a name and topic.
    • Switch to the My Groups tab to see groups you have joined or created.

    🚩 Reporting a User

    • On the Find Partners page, click Report on a partner card.
    • Describe the issue in the reason field and submit.
    • Reports are reviewed by administrators in the Admin Panel.
    • Your report is kept confidential — only admins can see who submitted it.

    ⚙️ Account & Profile

    • Edit your major, strengths, and weaknesses any time from My Profile.
    • If your account is SUSPENDED you cannot send messages or appear in searches, but you can still update your profile.
    • Contact an admin via the Messages feature to appeal a suspension.

    ❓ Common Issues

    I can't send messages
    Your account may be suspended. Check your profile status badge. Contact admin via Messages.
    I don't appear in partner searches
    Suspended accounts are hidden from searches. Also ensure you have set a Major in your profile.
    I'm not receiving notifications
    Notifications are polled every 5 seconds. Keep the app open and check your browser allows the page to stay active.
    I forgot my password
    Contact an administrator via the Messages feature to reset your password manually.

    📞 Contact Admin

    For technical issues or account help:

    1. Go to Messages in the sidebar.
    2. Find admin in the contacts list.
    3. Send a message describing your issue.

    Admins will reply directly in the chat.
